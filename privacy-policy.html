Expense Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

100% Private

Expense Tracker respects your financial privacy. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your expense data. All your financial information stays securely on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or financial information.

Expense Tracker stores all data locally on your device, including:

  • 💰 Expense amounts
  • 📝 Expense descriptions
  • 📂 Categories (Food, Transport, Shopping, etc.)
  • 📅 Transaction dates

This information is never uploaded to any server or shared with anyone.

Financial Data Security

Your financial data is sensitive, and we treat it with the utmost care:

  • All expense data is stored locally on your device
  • No cloud synchronization or backup to external servers
  • No access to your bank accounts or payment methods
  • No financial data is ever transmitted over the internet

Third-Party Services

Expense Tracker is a standalone application that does not integrate with:

  • Analytics or tracking services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Banking or payment services

Your expense data remains completely isolated and private.

Permissions

Expense Tracker requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your expense records locally

We do not request access to contacts, location, camera, or any other sensitive permissions.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual expenses anytime by swiping
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data
  • No data recovery possible after deletion (no backups)

Children's Privacy

Expense Tracker does not collect personal information from any users, including children. The app is suitable for users of all ages who want to track their spending.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a new revision date.
